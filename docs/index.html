Welcome to Sohail Sayeed's Website

Sohail Sayeed has been passionate about aviation and space since he was a child. He strives to become a guidance, navigation, and control expert for spacecraft and to work for a respected space organization such as NASA or SpaceX. Sohail started his journey by serving in the Air Force for six years as an Armament Maintenance Technician for F-16 fighter jets. He achived the supervisor rank as well as notable acheviements and medals such as the Afghanistan Campagin medal, the Northern Atlantic Treaty Organization medal, and two Air Force Achievement medals. Sohail is currently finishing up his Bachelors of Science in Computer Science at the University of Massachusetts-Boston, while also pursuing a private pilots certificate. In his free time he has a pletora of different hobbies and skills, as well as different leadership hobbies such as being an active volunteer and council member for Troop 19 of the Boy Scounts of America, and organization the largest board gaming group in Massachusetts south of Boston.
